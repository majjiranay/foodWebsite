Honey Barbeque Chicken

Crispy chicken peices coated in a sweet honey barbeque sause

Prep time: 30 minutes

Ingedients

  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 5 cups corn flakes cereal, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¾ cup barbecue sauce
  • ¼ cup honey

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a baking sheet with a silicone baking mat or parchment paper and set aside.
    2. Add cornflakes, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt and pepper to a medium bowl. Set aside.
    3. Whisk eggs and milk together in a small bowl.
    4. Begin by dipping chicken in flour then egg, then the crushed cornflakes. Repeat with all of the chicken bites.
    5. Place the breaded chicken bites on the prepared baking sheet.
    6. Bake for 8-10 minutes on each side flipping halfway through.
    7. Heat the barbecue sauce and honey in a small saucepan over medium heat.
    8. Toss to coat the chicken bites in the sauce and serve immediately.
